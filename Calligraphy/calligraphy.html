Calligraphy

Here I want to show you my Chinese calligraphy works, which I have been practicing since I was six years old. It has become an inseparable part of my identity. Chinses Calligraphy is true art - each stroke contains variations. My Master told me: when you write, you are having a conversation with ancient time.
