Haoyu Dong is a Ph.D. researcher from the Department of Industrial Design,Technische Universiteit Eindhoven, the Netherlands. She holds a Bachelor of Engineering in Transportation Design (Tongji Uni, China) and a Master of Science in Industrial Design (TU Eindhoven, Netherlands). Her current research focuses on human factor research and interaction design in the domain of automated mobility for facilitating trust relationships between human users and advanced technology and creating enjoyable user experiences. She retains the aspiration that technology should be grounded into our daily life and assist instead of replacing humans. In her work, she leverages the innovative and creative features of design activities and artifacts to conduct research.
