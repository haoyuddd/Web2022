Research Focus

So, yes, the topic of my Ph.D. is "interaction design in the highly automated vehicle to calibrate trust and enhance user experience". Let's be honest, the expectation for autonomous cars is overpromised, the final implementation relied more on the human factor aspects rather than the technology. I aim at connecting human users with automation technology through better usability and user experience, to fulfill the precondition of safety and then reach the ultimate acceptance.
